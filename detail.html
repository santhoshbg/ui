One of the newest hotels in Singapore, Village Hotel Sentosa offers 606 cosy guestrooms decorated in sun-washed hues of nature. Located in the heart of all the action, the hotel is just a walk away to Palawan and Tanjong Beach and all major tourist attractions in Sentosa. As our guest, you’ll get to live like a local in a global village. Imagine travelling through an Amazonian river and soaking in the Pamukkale with your loved ones. Or exploring the educational Eco Trail and discovering the rich heritage of the island before catching Sentosa’s spectacular sunset at the deck.
